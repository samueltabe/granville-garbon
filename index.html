Workshop on Energy Self-Production in Gabon

Launch of the National Programme for Energy Self-Supply (Auto-alimentation énergétique)

Wed, 15 Jan 2026
09:30 – 18:00
Radisson Blu, Libreville
Register for Workshop Download Invitation

Hosted by: Ministry of Universal Access to Water & Energy + Granville Energy

Setting a New Standard

Protocol of Agreement signed 06 October 2025 with the Ministry to support a national solar self-production programme.

This workshop serves as a vital information and exchange session for organisations with rooftop space to learn about solar + storage options, eligibility, and participation steps.

Core Objectives

Launch Programme

Officially launch and promote the national programme for energy self-supply in Gabon.

Educate & Explain

Detail solar/battery solutions, eligibility criteria, and the evaluation process.

Actionable Steps

Pre-screen participants for energy audits, MoUs, and future solar deployments.

Who Should Attend?

If you manage a facility with rooftop space and recurring electricity costs, this event is for you.

  • Government ministries and agencies
  • Hospitals, health facilities, universities
  • Local authorities (municipalities, councils)
  • Public and semi-public institutions
  • Industrial, mining, and commercial companies
Public & Private Sector

Workshop Topics

Main Topics

  • Key challenges and opportunities in Gabon
  • Technical solar & storage solutions
  • Financing mechanisms
  • Administrative procedures

Special Attention

  • Optimising available rooftop space
  • Reducing electricity drawn from the national grid
  • Understanding the "Auto-alimentation" framework
